ABOUT US

Veer Music Store was established by Late Sh.Hans Raj, who initiated the work in the field of making musical instruments in 1945 in his uncle's workshop at Lahore. He commenced his own business in 1962 from a small shop at New Delhi. S. Raj and Sanjeev, both the sons joined their father at teenage along with concentrating on their education, have now 30 years of vast experience in the field of musical instruments and leading the organization to the amazing heights.
